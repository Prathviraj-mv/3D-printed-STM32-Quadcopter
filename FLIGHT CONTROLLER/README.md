### 1. **MPU6050 Connections**
| MPU6050 Pin | Connect To STM32 |
|-------------|------------------|
| VCC         | 3.3V (not 5V)    |
| GND         | GND              |
| SCL         | PB6 (I2C1_SCL)   |
| SDA         | PB7 (I2C1_SDA)   |

---

### 2. **BMP180 Connections**
| BMP180 Pin  | Connect To STM32 |
|--------------|------------------|
| VCC          | 3.3V             |
| GND          | GND              |
| SCL          | PB6 (shared with MPU6050 SCL) |
| SDA          | PB7 (shared with MPU6050 SDA) |

---
### 3. **CHANNEL**

| CHANNEL        | PINS              |
|--------------|------------------|
| 1          | PB8             |
| 2          |  PA15          |
| 3          |  PB6 |
| 4          | PA7  |

---
### 3. **ESC**

| MOTOR        | ESC              |
|--------------|------------------|
| 1          | PA0           |
| 2          |  PA1          |
| 3          |  PB0 |
| 4          | PA6 |
---
